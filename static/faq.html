What is high cholesterol?
When there is too much cholesterol in your blood, it builds up in the walls of your arteries and increases your risk of developing heart disease. Check this site for more information.
What is HDL?
HDL is sometimes called good cholesterol. It carries cholesterol from other parts of your body back to your liver where it is then removed.
